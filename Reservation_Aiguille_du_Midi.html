Our advice:

Please present this document directly at the boarding gate.

Important: Departure 15 minutes after boarding - We ask you to prepare this document before the check-in (either on your smartphone or printed on paper or saved on your phone). No printing from the pick-up box.

ATTENTION: This document is not your ticket, you must present this document (dry booking) + your ticket.

Useful information:

  • -Access by two separate cable cars (65 places per cabin), journey time of 20 to 40 minutes, depending on the number of people.
  • -If you plan to stop at the intermediate station, we advise you to do so on your return.
  • -Bring warm clothes, sunglasses and sunscreen (even in summer).
  • -Average time for a visit of 2 to 3 hours (travel included). Please note that the visit time may be doubled depending on the numbers of visitors.
  • -It is not possible to book your return time while purchasing your ticket. On busy days, a schedule will be sent to you at the summit of the Aiguille du Midi by an operating agent (visit time limited from 1.30 hours to 2 hours depending on the number of visitors).
  • -Times and frequency of departures vary according to the time of year and the number of people: visit our website for more information and download the Chamonix app for live information.
  • -The visit is not recommended in case of severe cold or heart issues.
  • -The visit is prohibited for children under 3 years old, not recommended for children under 5 years old.
  • -Pets are prohibited.

Altitude effects: the altitude might have an impact on your body. In the event of an undesirable effect, do not hesitate to call on our staff who are formed accordingly.

Please be aware: Our sites may experience periods of closure (due to unfavorable weather, technical maintenance or annual closure).
